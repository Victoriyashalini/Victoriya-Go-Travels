Travel Guide

Day 1

Arrive at Ngurah Rai International Airport, where our representative will greet you and later board the vehicle for getting transferred to your hotel. Rest of the day will be free at leisure. Return back to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 2

Kickstart your morning with the scrumptious breakfast . Visit Kintamani Volcano, a rare opportunity to experience one of Bali’s last remaining active volcanos up close. The tour begins with the sights of a vibrant art village. Meet with the locals who keep the techniques of the region’s handicrafts alive with a stop at Tohpati village, widely known as the centre of handmade Balinese Batik weaving. Next stop is Celuk village famous for producing high-grade gold and silver handicrafts. Continue trip to Mas Village, one of the many artists villages surrounding Ubud, is the best place on the island to find high-quality Balinese woodcarvings, considered some of the best woodcarvings in the world. The tour continues to the Mount & Lake Batur in Kintamani village for some stunning views of the beautiful locales in this region. End your tour in one of the most famous coffee plantations in the area. Here you can look & taste many kinds of coffees from Indonesia. Drive past the Tegalalang village, with some of the most beautiful views of rice terraces. Upon enjoying your fun-filled day, return back to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 3

Wake up with a delicious breakfast at the hotel. Add an adrenaline kick to your Bali vacation with a choice of thrilling water sports and activities at Tanjung Benoa beach. Located in the Benoa peninsula far away from the hustle & bustle enjoy a fun banana boat ride, zip along the beach on a jet ski or try exhilarating water sports like parasailing. Thereafter visit the beautiful Uluwatu temple, renowned for its magnificent location, perched on top of a steep cliff approximately 70 metres above the waves. As one of the 6 key temples considered to be Bali's 'spiritual pillars', Uluwatu also offers a splendid sunset backdrop. It is definitely one of the top places in Bali for witnessing a delightful sunset, with direct views overlooking the beautiful waves of the Indian Ocean and daily Kecak dance performances (Entrance not included) held at an amphitheatre nearby. Return to your hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 4

Wake up in the morning, have a delicious breakfast at the hotel. Enjoy the day free at leisure exploring the surroundings on own. Today you can choose to head to Tanah Lot Temple (at an extra cost), probably the most popular and photographed temple on the island. Tanah Lot Temple stands for Earth (Tanah) and Sea (Lot), quite appropriate considering its spectacular setting on top of an impressive rock overlooking the Indian Ocean. At the entrance lane to Tanah Lot Temple, you will find a beautiful architectural Balinese gate and many art shops offering local handicrafts besides paintings, woodcarvings, and more. Overnight stay at your hotel. (Please note that these are suggested activities, supplement charges will be applicable).

Day 5

Wake up in the morning, have a scrumptious breakfast and pack your bags for your departure. Complete the check-out formalities and get ready to leave to the airport to catch a suitable flight to back home.
